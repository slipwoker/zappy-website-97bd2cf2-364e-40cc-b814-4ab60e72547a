Work Process

Three Simple Steps to Perfect Tile Finishing

Groutbot fits naturally with teams familiar with tiling work — without a steep learning curve, without material waste, and only consistent and professional results in every project.

01
First Step

Load the Material

Insert the grout cartridge into the device with one simple motion. The device is designed to accept standard grout materials common in the market — without additional tools, without special preparation.

02
Second Step

Guide Along the Joint

Guide the injection head along the grout joint between the tiles. The electric injection mechanism provides uniform and continuous pressure — an electric grout injection device for tiles that ensures precise depth and filling in every line.

03
Third Step

Clean and Consistent Finish

The result: uniform grout lines, less waste, and less cleaning time. Groutbot reduces manual effort and allows the team to progress faster — without compromising on finish quality.
